About Us

Charles and Catherine Neville purchased Devine-Hall & Associates, Ltd. in 2009. Although Devine-Hall & Associates, Ltd. had been in business for over 30 years with time-tested processes, a decision was made to change the name to Just Write Court Reporters to reflect our new energy and total commitment to our customers. The new owners wanted everything to be ‘just right’ for anyone doing business with their firm so the new name fit that attitude. Charles had previously worked in the auto insurance industry for nearly 20 years and felt that his experience in defense litigation would bring an expertise to the court reporting industry that was missing. He understands the desire of the legal community to be efficient, accurate and cost-effective. Just Write Court Reporters is dedicated to ensure our vision is reflected in every assignment.
